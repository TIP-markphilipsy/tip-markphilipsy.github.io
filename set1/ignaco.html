About Us

Who Am I?

Ms. Ignaco is a current Instructor and has been serving the institution for more than six years now. She was initially hired as a part-time faculty member in 2015 to teach computer programming, web development, and programming languages. To secure her tenure in the institution, in 2017, she applied for the plantilla position of Instructor I. In 2020, she was designated as a Coordinator of Research Services, and the year after she applied as a Faculty Researcher. Her interest in software development leads her contributions to the campus in the distribution of extension services in the community amid pandemics.

Being a graduate of MS Information Technology from Technological University of the Philippines- Manila, her research interests are focused on software development for the community service project. Among the researches, she conducted with her team are Incident Reporting Applications, Metal Working Industry E-Survey, GoTrace Contact Tracing, Research and Extension Service Portal , and One Virtual Accreditation Room with the content management system.
