About Us

Who Am I?

Ms. Rasdas is a current Professor and has been serving the institution for more than 14 years now. She was initially hired as contractual faculty member in 2007 to teach Computer System Architecture, Operating System, Design and Analysis of Algorithm and Digital Logic Design. She was given the chance to have temporary plantilla item as Instructor I in 2009. In 2013, the permanent plantilla as Instructor III item was awarded to her and promoted as Assistant Professor IV. Designated as Extension Unit Head of the CvSU Naic Campus and currently on her 7 years. Her experience as extension unit head leads her contributions to the campus in the implementation of extension services in the community with or without pandemics. Leads the following externally and internally funded extension projects: Community Based Production, Processing, and Marketing of Milkfish and Tilapia extension projects with 1.5 million pesos budget funded by CHED, Salin Impormasyon sa Teknolohiya at Kompyuter (SIKTeK) extension projects; a five-year project with 500,000 pesos budget from the Cavite State University (CvSU) Naic, and Information and Computer Technology (ICT) Transfer of “Mr. Paw”: An Interactive Android Application for Pawikan Hatchery and Conservation extension project with 40,000 pesos budget from CvSU Naic.

Being a graduate of MS Information Technology from Technological University of the Philippines- Manila, her research interests are focused on hardware and software development that contribute in analyzing the biogas sludge level of Nitrogen and Phosphorus for possible use as organic fertilizer. Among the researches, she conducted with her co-faculty are the “Holistic Evaluation Model for Training and Extension Services” Based from Kirkpatrick Model and Data Mining Applications, System Analyzer for Soil and Water Nitrates and Phosphates for Fish Ponds, Impact Assessment on Smoked Soft Boned Bangus Processing Technology, 2019 Citizen Satisfaction Index System of Rosario, Cavite, Community Needs Assessment in Bucana Malaki, Naic, Cavite.
